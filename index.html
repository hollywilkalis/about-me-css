Hi! I’m Shyama (rhymes with llama and the Y is silent). My background is in oil painting and graphic design, but after a decade of working in the graphic and interactive design fields I've shifted more into the art side of things.

I’ve been commissioned by: The New York Times, Apple, Wired, Cosmopolitan, The Atlantic, The Undefeated, Fast Company, Texas Monthly Magazine, Red Bull Music Academy, and many others.

If you’d like to get in touch, or need support for my iPhone Stickers, you can email me at art@shyamagolden.com
